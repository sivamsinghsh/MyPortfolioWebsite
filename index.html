About me

Hi, my name is Shivam and I am a Data Analyst. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in science(Information Technology, Hemvati Nandan Bahuguna Garhwal University Uttarakhand) and Master’s degree in(Computer Application, BBD University Lucknow).

Being an ambitious individual, I strongly believe in learning new technologies and analytical skills to conclude to optimized business solutions. I also believe in prioritizing understanding of the data model to get the best analytical view of data.

I am an experienced data analyst with over 2 years of experience in data analysis, visualization, and modeling. I have a strong understanding of the travel industry and its key performance indicators (KPIs), and I am skilled in statistical analysis, data mining, and machine learning.I am highly proficient in Python, R, SQL, and Tableau, and have strong experience in data mining, machine learning, statistical analysis, and data visualization.
